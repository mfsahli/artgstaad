The heart of ART GSTAAD

The Book

Art Gstaad — Aspekte der Sammlung. Before the gallery opens its doors, the collection speaks through a book. Conceived and designed by Erich Bernhard Sahli, it tells the story of the works, asks the questions they raise, and is offered free of charge for educational purposes.

Leaf through it — page by page, spread by spread.

Opening exhibition · September 2026

Can art answer?

An experimental exhibition. With works by artists, it attempts to provoke possible answers to questions — themes intended to playfully encourage self-reflection and an in-depth examination of the works.

The story so far

From a collection
to a gallery

Gsteig b. Gstaad sits at the head of the Saanenland, beneath the peaks that have drawn collectors, artists and connoisseurs to this valley for a century. ART GSTAAD brings the championship league of art to the mountains.

2016
The group is founded

ART GSTAAD — international art group. A private initiative to gather and document important works from international collectors.

2024 — 2026
The Book

Art Gstaad — Aspekte der Sammlung: the collection is told as a story, free for educational purposes. The flipbook edition follows.

September 2026
The gallery opens

Gsteig b. Gstaad. The opening exhibition asks: Can art answer? — with Max Ernst, Picasso, Lisa Brice, Marina Abramović, Gerhard Richter and others.
