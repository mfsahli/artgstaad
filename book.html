The story

Not a catalogue —
a conversation

Every collection has a story, and this one is told before the gallery doors even open. Art Gstaad — Aspekte der Sammlung, conceived, written and designed by Erich Bernhard Sahli, is the heart of ART GSTAAD: an art book that walks through the collection the way a visitor walks through a gallery — slowly, spread by spread, question by question.

Works by some of the most important artists of the twentieth and twenty-first centuries are placed side by side with questions that are direct, playful and sometimes provocative — the same questions that shape the opening exhibition, Can art answer?

The book is designed to be viewed as double-page spreads, the way it was composed, and can be obtained free of charge for educational purposes — an art lesson book for schools, students and anyone who wants to learn to look.
