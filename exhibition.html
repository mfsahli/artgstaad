The concept

An experimental exhibition

A new art exhibition in Gsteig / Gstaad is currently being realized. Important works of art from international collectors will be exhibited in the gallery.

With works by artists, the exhibition attempts to provoke possible answers to questions. The themes are intended to playfully encourage a kind of self-reflection and an in-depth examination of the works — the same questions posed in the ART GSTAAD book.
